# Datastructures:

buttons can be mapped to game actions

devices are split into 3 groups such as:
 
- kb/ mouse
- gamepad
- joysticks

a single action cannot be mapped to more than 1 button per group of devices.

All actions are exported from the game, and they have unique ids, as well as activation modes and categories
